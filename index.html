Clear Your Browser Cache

Keep your browsing smooth and efficient!

Why Clear Your Cache?

Clearing your browser cache helps improve performance, fix loading issues, and ensure youâ€™re viewing the latest version of websites.

Desktop Instructions

Google Chrome

  1. Click on the three dots in the upper right corner.
  2. Navigate to More Tools > Clear Browsing Data.
  3. Select Cached images and files.
  4. Choose a time range (e.g., Last hour, All time).
  5. Click Clear data.

Mozilla Firefox

  1. Click on the three horizontal lines in the upper right corner.
  2. Select Options > Privacy & Security.
  3. Scroll down to Cookies and Site Data and click Clear Data.
  4. Check Cached Web Content and click Clear.

Microsoft Edge

  1. Click on the three dots in the upper right corner.
  2. Go to Settings > Privacy, search, and services.
  3. Under Clear browsing data, click Choose what to clear.
  4. Select Cached images and files and click Clear now.

Mobile Instructions

Google Chrome (Android/iOS)

  1. Open Chrome and tap the three dots (menu).
  2. Go to History > Clear browsing data.
  3. Select Cached images and files.
  4. Choose a time range and tap Clear data.

Safari (iOS)

  1. Go to Settings > Safari.
  2. Scroll down and tap Clear History and Website Data.
  3. Confirm by tapping Clear History and Data.

Regularly clearing your cache helps keep your browsing experience smooth and efficient!
